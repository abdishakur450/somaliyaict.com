Welcome to Cybersecurity Learning

In today's digital age, understanding cybersecurity is crucial for everyone, especially students who are constantly online. This hub provides engaging resources, best practices, and interactive labs to help you stay safe in the cyber world.

Learn how to protect your data, recognize threats, and build secure habits!

Key Cybersecurity Best Practices

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords and a password manager.

Multi-Factor Authentication

Enable MFA on all accounts for extra security.

Regular Updates

Keep your software and devices updated to patch vulnerabilities.

Avoid Phishing

Be cautious with emails and links from unknown sources.

Interactive Labs for Students

Lab 1: Password Strength Challenge

Lab 2: Setting Up MFA

Lab 3: Phishing Detection Game

Identify phishing emails in simulated scenarios and learn to spot red flags.
