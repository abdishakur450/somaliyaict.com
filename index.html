Introduction to Cybersecurity Best Practices

Cybersecurity is crucial in today's digital world. This page provides an overview of best practices and hands-on labs to help you understand and implement them.

Key Best Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts.
  • Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) wherever possible.
  • Keep software and systems up to date with the latest patches.
  • Be cautious with email attachments and links from unknown sources.
  • Regularly back up important data.
  • Use antivirus software and firewalls.
  • Educate yourself on phishing and social engineering tactics.
